— Our story —

Built on 30 years
of experience.

Angimen Holding was born from the vision of George Kahil and a trajectory of three decades linked to the real estate, construction, renovation and maintenance sectors in Marbella and the Costa del Sol.

We have worked alongside owners, communities, businesses and international clients who needed more than just a provider: they needed someone local, serious and capable of coordinating every detail.

We know Marbella, its most exclusive areas, its properties, its rhythms and its demands. We also know the right professionals to execute each task with judgement, discretion and follow-through.

— How we work —

A close, discreet
and professional approach.

So your property always moves in the right direction.

01

We listen

We understand what you need, what worries you and what your goal is with the property: buying, renovating, maintaining, managing or preparing for sale.

02

We bring order

We review the situation, identify priorities and define what should be done first to avoid improvised decisions.

03

We design the plan

We prepare a clear, realistic proposal tailored to your property, with concrete actions, timelines and the professionals needed.

04

We coordinate for you

We organise vendors, technical teams, viewings, works and incidents so you don't have to be on top of every detail.

05

We supervise each phase

We control execution, review progress and maintain direct communication so you always know where everything stands.

06

We care over time

Once the work is finished, we can continue to support you with management, maintenance and set-up throughout the year.
